# Card Engine Codex v1  
## Section 05 — Synergies and Combos

---

### 5.1 Purpose

Define how individual cards combine into patterns, synergies, and combo lines that reward planning, timing, and affinity alignment.

---

### 5.2 Synergy Types

- **Type Synergy** — OFFENSE + RHYTHM, DEFENSE + CORRUPTION, etc.
- **Pulse Synergy** — Effects that trigger only on specific beat windows.
- **Zone Synergy** — Cards that gain power in specific zones.
- **Affinity Synergy** — Operative Affinity interacting with card tags.

---

### 5.3 Combo Structure

Describe:

- Combo steps
- Required conditions
- Rewards / payoffs

---

### 5.4 Risks and Constraints

Prevent degenerate loops and infinite combos.
